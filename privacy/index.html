Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Heal360 Wellness. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and sensitive information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us to deliver personalized wellness and care services.

a) Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address

This information is used for authentication, communication, and account management.

b) Health & Medical Information (User-Provided)

After signing in, you may voluntarily provide health-related information, including but not limited to:

  • Medical history and fertility-related details
  • Lifestyle and wellness information
  • Reports or documents uploaded by you
  • Information shared during consultations

This data is collected only when you choose to share it and is used solely for providing personalized care and services.

c) Usage Data

We may collect limited non-personal information such as app usage patterns to improve app performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage our services
  • Enable secure login and account access
  • Personalize wellness, consultation, and care recommendation
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, consultations, or updates
  • Maintain internal records for continuity of care

We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

Heal360 Wellness does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information.

We may use trusted service providers (such as Google Workspace tools including Google Meet and Calendar) strictly to:

  • Schedule and conduct consultations
  • Manage internal operations securely

These services are used only as tools and do not have independent rights to your data.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Access to sensitive information is limited to authorized Heal360 personnel only.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as your account remains active or as required to provide services.

Your Right to Delete

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
